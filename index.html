Our Story

The Lisbon Grill offers an extensive menu, filled with different variety. We also love to serve our food in a family-style setting. You can always order your favorite dishes for two and split it with your loved ones.

Our restaurant is all about family. We love having a place where everyone can come and have a good time. We even have our own kids-menu for all of the wonderful children. Our goal is to give you a little taste of Portugal and have you leaving with a smile on your face. Book your reservation today!
